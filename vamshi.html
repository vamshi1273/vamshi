Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Swasthya Dwar.

What is Swasthya Dwar?

Swasthya Dwar is a digital health record management platform designed specifically for migrant workers. It helps you maintain and access your health records anytime, anywhere.

How do I update my health information?

You can update your health information by going to the "My Health Profile" section. Click on the edit button next to the information you want to update.

What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

In case of a medical emergency, press the SOS button on the home screen. This will notify your emergency contacts and display your health information for medical personnel.

How do I schedule an appointment?

Go to the Appointments section and click on "Schedule New Appointment". Select your preferred hospital, doctor, and time slot.

Is my health data secure?

Yes, we use advanced encryption and security measures to protect your health data. Your information is only accessible to you and authorized medical personnel during emergencies.
